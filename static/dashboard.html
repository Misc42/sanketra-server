Privacy contract

Kahan rehta hai tera data?

Storage: SQLite file at ~/.config/sanketra/history.db on this PC.

Encryption: Filesystem-level only. DPDP-acceptable. No per-row encryption because the DB never leaves your disk.

What's sent over the network: Only between your PC and your phone, on your Wi-Fi. Never to us. Never to anyone else.

How to clear: Settings tab → "Clear all history" (double-confirm). Or delete the .db file directly.

Export: Settings tab → Export as JSON/TXT/Markdown. Portable. Yours.

"If a feature makes PC voice input better, we build it. If it sends your data somewhere, we don't."
